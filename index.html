The Development

Where every home is a vision realised.

Perched on the gentle rise of Taman Wawasan Mahandoi in Donggongon, Penampang, Vision Garden 築夢園 is a masterplanned community that has grown, phase by phase, into one of the district's most sought-after addresses.

Phase 5 crowns the development with just 43 double storey terrace houses — architecturally sculpted gable roofs, generous 20-foot frontages, and light-filled interiors designed for the way modern Sabahan families truly live. Moments from the Pan Borneo Highway, yet wrapped in green.
